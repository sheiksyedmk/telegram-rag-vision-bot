# Remote Work Policy

## Overview
This policy covers when and how employees may work remotely. It applies to all employees whose roles allow remote work.

## Eligibility
- Roles that require physical presence (e.g., front desk, facilities) are not eligible.
- Managers decide if a role is suitable for remote work.

## Remote Work Schedule
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Remote days should be agreed in advance and visible in the shared team calendar.
- Core meetings must be attended on time, regardless of location.

## Availability and Communication
- Employees must be reachable via chat, email, and phone during core working hours.
- Status in communication tools should accurately reflect availability.
- Video should be on for most internal meetings unless agreed otherwise.

## Equipment and Security
- Employees are responsible for a stable internet connection.
- Company devices must be used for all work activities where possible.
- Confidential data must not be viewed in public spaces where screens are easily visible.

## Questions
For questions about remote work eligibility or setup, contact your manager or IT.
