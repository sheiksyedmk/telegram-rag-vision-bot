# Leave Policy

## Overview
This policy explains how employees can take paid time off, sick leave, and unpaid leave. It applies to all full‑time employees.

## Annual Leave
- Employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Leave is accrued monthly and visible in the HR system.
- Leave must be requested at least 7 days in advance, except in emergencies.
- Managers can decline leave requests if they create critical staffing gaps.

## Sick Leave
- Employees should inform their manager as soon as possible when they are sick.
- A medical certificate may be requested for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.
- Sick leave is not deducted from annual leave balances.

## Unpaid Leave
- Unpaid leave may be granted at the manager’s discretion for special situations.
- Unpaid leave does not accrue benefits and does not count toward years of service.

## Public Holidays
- Company‑recognized public holidays are listed in the HR system.
- Employees do not need to apply for leave on these days.

## Questions
For questions about this policy, contact HR or your manager.
